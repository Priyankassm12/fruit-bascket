FRUIT BASCKET
We simply can’t get enough floral photography, but we also love to read beautiful poetry and prose about flowers and nature. In just a few powerful words, our mind creates its own image and invigorates our senses, whisking us away to that endless flower field or basking in the sun with our hands in the garden. There’s just something so intimate about letting our imaginations run freely after diving into a poem, so we wanted to share our favorite flower poetry with you. Hopefully, these beautiful words inspire your own imaginations and possibly a poem of your own! Beauty in nature Flower blooms so succulent Hummingbirds flutter -Carol Brown One little flower Nods in the delicate breeze As the crickets hum -Jeanine DeJesus.
